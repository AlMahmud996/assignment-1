Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Introducing new ideas and solutions that improve our lives. It drives growth, enhances efficiency, and keeps the world moving forward.

Sustainability

Focuses on meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs. Balancing environmental, social, and economic factors to ensure long-term well-being and health for our planet and its inhabitants.

Infrastructure

Well-planned and sustainable infrastructure is crucial for economic growth, social development, and environmental protection, ensuring that communities thrive now and in the future.

Empowerment

Fosters self-reliance, promotes equality, and enables people to contribute actively to their communities, driving positive change and personal growth.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

  • Poverty is not created by the poor. It is created by the system we have built, the institutions we have designed, and the concepts we have formulated. These structures, shaped over time by policies and practices, often marginalize and exclude the most vulnerable populations. They perpetuate cycles of deprivation by limiting access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
    We must recognize that poverty is a systemic issue, not an individual failing. By redesigning our institutions and rethinking our societal values, we can create a more inclusive world where everyone has the chance to thrive. It requires collective action, innovative thinking, and a commitment to justice and equality. Only then can we dismantle the barriers that create poverty and build a future where prosperity is shared by all."

  • Dr. Muhammad Yunus

  • Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
